# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or
sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but respect boundaries when asked to change behavior.
- Focus feedback on ideas, code, and documentation rather than people.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sustained disruption, doxxing, or
other conduct that would reasonably make someone feel unsafe or unwelcome is not
acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or
restrict participation for behavior that violates this code of conduct.

To report a concern, contact security@powerset.co.
