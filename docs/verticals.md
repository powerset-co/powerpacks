# Verticals

Powerpacks V1 has two public verticals.

## `people_by_role`

Use when the request is primarily about who someone is:

- title
- function
- track
- seniority
- location

Examples:

- software engineers in sf
- principals in data
- vp product at stripe

## `people_by_company`

Use when the request is primarily about where someone works:

- type of company
- sector
- funding stage
- company geography

Examples:

- engineers at ai startups
- operators at series a fintech companies
- founders at developer tools companies in sf
